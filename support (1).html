Draft. Spin. Dominate. Here's how everything works — and how to reach us.

How do I play?

Pick a sport, spin to draft players onto your lineup, then simulate a full season and see how your squad does. Compete for the best team rating on the global leaderboard.

What are lives, and why are there ads?

The free version uses lives — you start with 5, spend one to start a new game, and they refill over time (one every 30 minutes). When you're out, you can watch a short ad for an extra life, wait for them to refill, or remove ads entirely.

How do I remove ads?

Tap Remove Ads on the home screen (or in Profile). It's a one-time $4.99 purchase that turns off all ads and makes lives unlimited.

I bought Remove Ads but it's gone after reinstalling.

Open the app, go to the upgrade/Remove Ads screen, and tap Restore Purchases. Your purchase will be re-applied at no charge.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Delete account. This permanently deletes your account and all associated data and cannot be undone.
