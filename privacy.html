Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ball Knowledge ("we", "us", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile app and website (ballknowledgesports.com). By using Ball Knowledge, you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

  • Account information. When you create an account, we collect your email address and a username you choose. On the website, you may instead sign in with Google, in which case Google provides your email address and basic profile name.
  • Gameplay data. We store the teams you draft, your game and season results, achievements, and your leaderboard entries so your progress is saved and rankings can be displayed.
  • Advertising & device data (iOS). To show ads in the free version, our advertising partner (Google AdMob) may collect a device advertising identifier, IP address, and app-usage signals. See "Advertising" below.
  • Purchase information. Purchases are processed by Apple (in the app) or Stripe (on the website). We receive confirmation that a purchase occurred but do not receive your full card or payment details.

How we use information

  • To provide the game, save your progress, and display leaderboards.
  • To show ads in the free version and to process the optional "Remove Ads" purchase.
  • To respond to support requests and keep the service secure and working.

Third-party services

We rely on the following providers, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase — account authentication, database, and storage.
  • Google AdMob — advertising in the free iOS version.
  • Google Sign-In — optional login on the website.
  • Apple — in-app purchases on iOS.
  • Stripe — payments on the website.

Advertising

The free iOS version shows ads through Google AdMob, which may use a device identifier to serve and measure ads. You can limit ad personalization in your device's privacy settings, and you can remove ads entirely with the one-time "Remove Ads" purchase in the app.

Data retention and deletion

You can permanently delete your account at any time from Profile → Delete account inside the app. This removes your account and the associated data (saved teams, stats, achievements, and leaderboard entries). You may also email us to request deletion.

We keep your information while your account is active. Some records may persist briefly in backups before being overwritten.

Children's privacy

Ball Knowledge is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can exercise account deletion directly in the app, or contact us for other requests.

Security

We use reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

Contact
